Earn Your Encore Karaoke

A simple, exciting vote-to-continue format

Singers get a warm-up song, then the room votes on their next one. There’s no “overall winner” — just a fun, fair rotation driven by the crowd. KJ runs the show; we simply provide the voting.

Host Controls Everything

The KJ sets the venue, host name, and current singer, starts the vote, and ends the round. Quick buttons allow host votes too.

Your laptop is connected to the venue audio; all sounds (vote open / results) play from here.

Big Screen Display

Shows “Welcome to” with the public venue name, “Hosted by” the KJ, and the current singer. During voting it animates incoming votes; after, it displays the result card.

Encore!Another Shot!Maybe Next Time!

Simple Voter Page

Audience members enter the event PIN once, then tap to vote. One vote per device per singer is enforced.

Live Momentum

As votes come in, animated dots hit the columns on the big screen. Everyone can feel the energy build.

Encore!

Encore winners stay in the rotation. Another Shot singers can be re-inserted later. “Maybe Next Time” singers politely exit for the night.

It keeps the show tight, fun, and rewarding for strong performers — while still giving everyone a great time.

Hype Moments

Occasional on-screen splashes like “Make Some Noise!” keep the crowd engaged between songs.

The Board

A clean board view shows the most recent Encore! and Another Shot! singers — easy for the KJ and crowd to follow.

Finish Strong

End the night with a quick “Thanks for coming out!” message. The rotation remains friendly — come back and try again!
