Encore! Voting

Give Your Crowd a Voice — Keep Your Show Moving

A simple, live voting flow that keeps great singers coming back and engages the whole room. Works with your existing KJ setup. No winner—just Encore!, Another Shot!, or Maybe Next Time!

Encore! Host Controls the Show

The Encore! Host (often the KJ) manages the event: set the venue and host name, pick the current singer, start and end voting, and see live results at a glance. Quick-access buttons keep the show flowing.

Encore! can be run by the KJ or by a dedicated Encore! operator (available in our local service areas). KJs can register, subscribe, and add Encore! Nights to their existing karaoke shows anytime.

Requires Internet access. Offline mode coming soon!

Beautiful Big-Screen Display

The main screen welcomes guests, shows the host/venue, and highlights who’s up. During voting, a timer and live tally keep the room focused and engaged.

Clean, readable layouts work great on TVs and projectors.

One-Tap Voting on Phones

Guests join with the event PIN and tap to vote: Encore!, Another Shot!, or Maybe Next Time!

We enforce one vote per device per singer to keep it fair.

Live Votes That Feel Alive

Every vote animates in as a colored ball—green for Encore!, yellow for Another Shot!, red for Maybe Next Time!. The room can literally see the momentum!

Celebrate the Moment

When time’s up, the result shows for a short burst—Encore!, Another Shot!, or Maybe Next Time!. No single “winner”—just a smooth rotation that rewards crowd favorites.

Built-In Hype & Prompts

Need a pop of energy? Fire a quick splash like “Make Some Noise!” or drop a “Thanks for coming out!” at the end of the night.

The Encore Board

A clean board view shows recent Encore! and Another Shot! results at a glance—perfect for a side TV.

End on a High Note

Wrap the night with a branded “Thanks” screen and keep guests excited for the next Encore! Night.
